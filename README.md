# AI-ML-Collab

Books, articles, and other resources for AI, ML, Data Science, and Analytics.

Welcome to the AI-ML-Collab wiki!

The AI-ML Collaborative Nexus is an NYU-based academic research group that unites a diverse community of researchers and academicians from various departments across New York University and the NYU School of Medicine. This interdisciplinary group is dedicated to leveraging the combined power of machine learning, artificial intelligence, and advanced statistical methods to tackle critical research problems spanning multiple domains, including healthcare, technology, bioinformatics, environmental science, mental and behavioral health, and data science. By bridging these departments, the Nexus aims to cultivate a robust network of expertise that drives innovation at the intersection of computational methodology and practical research application.

The central goals of the AI-ML Collaborative focus on collective growth, resource sharing, and structured experimentation. The group strives to break down professional and academic silos, creating a supportive, non-competitive environment for members with varying skill levels to enhance their proficiency in AI/ML tools. The AI-ML Collab facilitates innovation through peer-to-peer knowledge exchange, case study workshops, and a detailed skills and resource inventory. Ultimately, the group seeks to move beyond theoretical discussions to identify and develop practical use cases and proof-of-concept projects where advanced AI-driven solutions can significantly improve research workflows and outcomes across the group's diverse areas of study.
